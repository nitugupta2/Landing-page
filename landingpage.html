1. Compelling Headline

Clear and Concise: The headline should immediately convey the value proposition

Attention-Grabbing: It should capture the visitor's attention and make them want to learn more.

2. Engaging Subheadline

Supportive: The subheadline should support the headline by providing additional context or information.

3. Call to Action (CTA)

Prominent: The CTA button or link should be highly visible and stand out from the rest of the page.
Action-Oriented: Use action verbs like "Get Started," "Sign Up," or "Download Now" to prompt immediate action.
Above the Fold: Ensure the CTA is placed where visitors can see it without scrolling.
